# excel-retail-sales-dashboard
Excel-based retail sales analysis and dashboard
Retail Sales Analysis Project (Excel)
This Repo contains an **Excel-based retail sales analysis project** focused on identifying Revenue trends, Customer behavior and Product performance using various **Pivot tables** and **Visualization** techniques.

## Overview:
  The objective of this project was to perform exploratory data analysis on a retail sales dataset and present key findings through a compact and interactive **Excel Dashboard**.

## Problem Statements:
  1.	Which Months generate the highest and lowest revenue, and are there any noticeable seasonal patterns?
  2.	Which Product Categories contribute the most to total revenue?
  3.	Is Performance driven more by Price or Quantity sold?
  4.	How does average customer spending vary by Gender and Product Category?
  5.	Which Product Category shows consistent performance throughout the year compared to others?

## Dataset Description:
  -	Retail sales transactional data
  -	Fields include:
    -	Transaction ID
    -	Order Date
    -	Customer Age
    -	Product Category
    -	Gender 
    -	Quantity
    -	Price Per Unit
    -	Total Amount
  -	Each customer appears only once in the dataset with their respective {CUST000} ID. (Single transaction per customer)
  Data Source: [Public retail sales dataset sourced from Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mohammadtalib786/retail-sales-dataset)

## Analysis Workflow:
  The analysis was done entirely in **Microsoft Excel** and includes the following steps:
    -	Data quality checks (null values, date validation & formatting)
    -	Verification of Total Amount field by recalculating Quantity x Price Per Unit
    -	Creation of **Pivot Tables** to analyze:
        -	Monthly revenue trends
        -	Product Category performance
        -	Gender-wise spending patterns
        -	Age band distribution
        -	Average spending by Gender
    -	Calculation of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
    -	Development of a structured dashboard with visuals and summary insights.

## Key Insights:
  -	May recorded the highest revenue, while September experienced a slight dip in sales compared to other months.
  -	Electronics emerged as the top revenue-gathering Product category.
  -	Male customers contributed a larger part of revenue within the Electronics category.
  -	The Beauty product category showed the highest average spend among Male customers. 
  -	Clothing category sales remained relatively stable throughout the year with minor seasonal changes.
  
## Key Metrics:
  -	KPI cards for Total Revenue and Average Order Value, Best Category & Best Month.
  -	Monthly revenue trend Visualization.
  -	Category-wise and Gender-wise revenue analysis.
  -	Slicers of Months, Gender & Product Category for interactive Filtering.
  -	Insight text boxes highlighting key observations.
